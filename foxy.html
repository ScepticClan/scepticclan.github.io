FoxyFNCS is a gaming channel that showcases the skills and humor of a Fortnite player. FoxyFNCS uploads videos and shorts of his gameplay, challenges, and records on Fortnite. He also streams live on Twitch and interacts with his fans. FoxyFNCS has 45 subscribers and 10 videos on his channel. He is known for his fast and creative builds, his duo chemistry, and his Halloween-themed content. FoxyFNCS invites you to join his community and watch him dominate the game.
